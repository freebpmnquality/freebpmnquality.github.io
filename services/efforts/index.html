BASIC CONSTRUCTIVE COST MODEL

The efforts equation is as follows:

E = a × ∑(KLOC)b

Where:

  • E is the effort applied in person-months
  • KLOC is the estimated thousands of lines of code delivered for the project
  • a = 2.4 and b = 1.05 are model coefficients
See more about COCOMO
CONSTANT PRODUCT MARKET MAKER

The price in ETH is calculated as follows:

X × Y = K

Where:

  • X and Y stand for the number of tokens in the pool
  • K is a constant
  • we have Y for the price of 1 ETH in USD and K = 1, so X is the price of 1 USD in ETH
  • then Cost (in ETH) = E × 152 (hours per person-month) × 28 (the average hourly wage for a HTML developer) × X
